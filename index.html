A Star Search Visualization

H: Heuristic

This is the guiding component of the search. The heuristic is the calculation of distance from current position to the goal.

Euclidian allows for straight and diagonal movements.

Manhattan allows only for straight movements.

G: Move

The distance from the start position to the current position.

F: Cost

Cost is calculated by adding H and G.

The most efficient route is determined by choosing the lowest F score until reaching the goal.

Color representation

Black represents a wall, otherwise an invalid position.

Blue represents a calculated position in pursuit of finding the best route.

Orange represents the best path.

White represents an untested position. The F value is not efficient enough over the other positions.
